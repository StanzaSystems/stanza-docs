# Creating Environments

An environment of servers/deployments you maintain, either for production or for testing. 'dev', 'qa', 'prod', etc.

1. From the left hand side menu, select "Administration". 
1. Select the 'environments' tab.
1. Click on ![](/addenvironment.png)
1. Add a unique Name and Save.
