
# Creating Decorators

1. From the left hand side menu, select "Decorators". 
1. Click on ![](/adddecorator.png)
1. Add a unique Name. 
1. Select the correct [Environment](/glossary#environment), [Project](/glossary#project), [Traffic Type](/glossary#decorator) and click Save.
![](/createdecorator.png)
1. You'll be redirected to your Decorator's details page.
